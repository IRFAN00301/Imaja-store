Refund Policy

At Imaja Collections, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, you may be eligible for a refund as per the policy outlined below.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Refunds are only applicable for products that are damaged, defective, or incorrect upon delivery.
  • Requests must be submitted within 7 days of receiving the order.
  • The item must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with tags attached.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Visit the Returns & Cancellation page and fill out the request form.
  • Include your Order ID, email, and a clear reason for the refund request.
  • Attach photos of the item if it's damaged or defective.

Refund Process

  • Once your request is received and verified, we will notify you of the approval or rejection.
  • If approved, the refund will be processed within 5–7 business days via your original payment method.
  • In case of Cash on Delivery orders, bank details will be required for refund transfer.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Items bought on discount or during sale periods are not eligible for refund.
  • Customized or altered items cannot be returned or refunded.
